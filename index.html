Summary

Erica Schaub is a Vancouver-based Senior Graphic Designer with over a decade of experience in both agency and client-side environments, demonstrating expertise in design leadership, brand strategy, and product launches. With a specialized focus on the entertainment industry and e-commerce, she has developed a proven track record of building compelling, strategy-driven brands. Her extensive skill set includes art direction, brand identity, user experience (UX) and user interface (UI) design, as well as proficiency in various software tools such as Adobe Creative Suite and Figma.


Recent Roles

Most recently, Erica served as a Senior Brand Designer at EA Sports, where she collaborated with cross-functional teams on the development of comprehensive design systems for renowned brands like UFC and NHL. She played a key role in creating in-game and marketing assets, including product packaging and promotional materials, while ensuring adherence to brand guidelines. Additionally, Erica provided mentorship to junior designers and worked closely with specialty printers to maintain color accuracy across various mediums.

Previously, Erica held significant roles at Monstercat, where she led the art direction for the “Instinct” sub-label and launched a successful apparel line in collaboration with visual artists. Her innovative approach contributed to a 53% increase in sales for the Monstercat Shop, transforming it into a recognized fashion brand. Throughout her career, she has also worked as a freelance designer, developing brand systems for startups and providing design support to marketing agencies, further showcasing her versatility and commitment to enhancing creative workflows across industries.
